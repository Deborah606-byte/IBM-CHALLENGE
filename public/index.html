Benefits Of Agriculture

Source of Raw Materials

It is the main source for raw materials being it sugarcane or wood which are important to major industries for manufacturing purposes.

Beneficial for International Trading

Countries export agriultural products and some import them also. If a nation's agriculture suffers for some reason, prices may rise and the flow of commerce may be disrupted.

Agriculture Generates Revenue For The State

It helps in increasing a nation's revenue when products are exported.

A Source Of Employment

Be it a farmer, harvester, technician, scientist, there are many job opportunities in this field. Agricuture help reduce high rate of unemployment also help reduce poverty.

Profitable For Economic Development

The economic development is greatly tied to the agricultural sector. A nation benefits from decreased poverty and accelerated economic growth when trade, national income, and employment are combined in a good way. One of the finest methods to hasten development and raise a nation's standing internationally is improvement in agriculture.

Enhancing Our Traditional Methods

In order to eliminate poverty and support the first,second and eighth pillars of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), we want to employ agriculture. Increasing agricultural production and generating jobs along the agricultural value chain are now essential given that Ghana's present population is roughly 30 million.

Nearly half of Ghana's population works in agriculture, which is primarily agrarian. About 44.7% of the country's active work force and 83% of rural households rely heavily on the industry for their livelihoods.

The traditional methods of farming although has sustained the agriculture sector will no longer be adeqaute since the will be increased in population in the nearby future. It is our aim to help improve this traditional methods using technology so the agriculture sector can be boosted.
