Enhancing Our Traditional Methods

In order to eliminate poverty and support the first,second and eighth pillars of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), we want to employ agriculture. Increasing agricultural production and generating jobs along the agricultural value chain are now essential given the Ghana's present population of roughly 30 million.

Nearly half of Ghana's population works in agriculture, which is primarily agrarian. About 44.7% of the country's active work force and 83% of rural households rely heavily on the industry for their livelihoods.

The traditional methods of farming although has sustained the agriculture sector will no longer be adeqaute since the will be increased in population in the nearby future. It is our aim to help improve this traditional methods using technology so the agriculture sector can be boosted.
