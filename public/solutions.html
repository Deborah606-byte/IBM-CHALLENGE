Irrigation & Fertilizer Application Purposes

Types of irrigation: gravity, sprinkler and micro for efficiency and to save water
a. Sprinkler system: revolving nozzles are attached to pipelines, and water droplets are sprayed or sprinkled across the landscape like raindrops.
- Helps plants to be protected from harsh conditions
- This system can be used to apply fertilizers and pesticides
Tools that can be used: Drones with AI can be used for this purpose
b. Drip system: water flows through pipelines buried in the ground and drips through small holes (emitters) near plant roots.
- For plant health, direct watering of plant roots is necessary
- Conservation of water through soil moisture
Tools that can be used: software with AI to regulate water in pipelines
