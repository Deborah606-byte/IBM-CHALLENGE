Diagnosing weather patterns

It is our goal to help farmers predict accurate weather conditions before farming, to ensure they plant the right crops at the right time.
Temperature, humidity, Air pressure, precipitation, wind direction and wind speed among others will also be meaured and analyzed correctly to help ensure a sustainable productivity.
Farmers will also get advices on the information about the weather and temperature needed for each crop.
Temperature and avaiable light are two most important factors when determining planting time.

Testing Of Soil

Test for pH level, soil moisture, salinity, chemical content, structure and texture.
Checking the nutrient contents of the soil.
Based on this we will predict what to grow on that land for productivity for the farmers.
The purpose of soil testing is to accurately analyze the soil's fertility so that fertilizer recommendations can be made. Soil tests can also be used to indicate where fertilizers or manure shouldn't be applied due to the growing knowledge of fertilizer effects on environmental quality.

Crop Or Seed Planting

Ways to ensure farmers plant
For precision, the best ways for planting each type of crop, in terms of spaces, depth, arrangement
This will help improve crop stability and help reduce the spread if deiseases or pests when one crop gets infected. It also helps improve crop productivity.
Farmers will be educated on the importance of using the right patterbs for farming rather than using the old patterns of planting and end up loosing everything they planted to pests and diseases.

Irrigation & Fertilizer Application Purposes

According to our research, we found types of irrigation for modern practices: gravity, sprinkler and micro for efficiency and to save water
a. Sprinkler system: revolving nozzles are attached to pipelines, and water droplets are sprayed or sprinkled across the landscape like raindrops.
b. Drip system: water flows through pipelines buried in the ground and drips through small holes (emitters) near plant roots.

Monitoring Crops Or Health Assessment

To check the conditions of crops and ensure each crop is not infected.
Vast fields need such tools because of their size.
To help assess crops’ health by scanning them. This is to help stop the spread of infections in the entire crops to enable use fertilizers or pesticides at the right time and when needed.
